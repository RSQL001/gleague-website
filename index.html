Welcome to
G-LEAGUE

The Gamers League, G-League, is an e-sports organization that was launched in 2017. Operating out of Abuja, we are the first registered e-sports outfit in Nigeria. Our goal is to create a community of gamers who come together and compete in gaming tournaments, and the promotion of e-sports in Africa. Since our launch, our membership has been steadily increasing, with over 300+ active members today.


We host tournaments, gaming party's, and sporting events throughout the year. We are always giving out prize pools, gifts and much more to our members. Subscribe and join us today to be part of one of Africa's fastest growing e-sports and gaming communities.
